Ethiopia Clean Water Initiative (ECWI)

Bringing Clean Water, Building Bright Futures

Our Mission

At ECWI, our mission is to transform lives in rural Ethiopia by delivering affordable, sustainable, and innovative clean water solutions. We go beyond just providing access—we champion health, education, economic empowerment, and lasting change.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every Ethiopian, regardless of where they live, enjoys safe, clean, and accessible water—free from the threat of waterborne diseases.

Who We Are

ECWI is a non-profit organization on the frontlines of Ethiopia’s water crisis. We work hand-in-hand with communities to drill deep boreholes, install solar-powered pumps, distribute water filtration kits, and run hygiene workshops—all to improve lives and empower women and children.
